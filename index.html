Day 11 — TOTAL SOLAR ECLIPSE 🌑

Wednesday 12 August · totality 17:43 – 17:50
15:00 rave → 17:43 totalityOnce in a lifetime

Echolalia — Björk, Arca & the dark

Víðistaðatún, Hafnarfjörður · partial begins ~16:42 · TOTALITY 17:43–17:50 · 7 minutes

The day the whole trip orbits. From 3pm: Echolalia — Björk (DJ set), Arca (DJ set), Ronja, Sideproject — building to the moment the sun goes out over Iceland for seven minutes, one of the longest totalities of the century. Quiet morning first (National Gallery, Laufásvegur 12, is the gentle option).

Kit: eclipse glasses (no improvising), layers — the temperature drops fast in totality — and a charged phone for the transport squeeze afterwards. Sort transport to Víðistaðatún in advance; everyone in Iceland is moving that afternoon.
